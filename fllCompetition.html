FLL Regional Competition in Turku - 2025

About the Event

The regional First Lego League (FLL) competition was held in Turku, Finland at the prestigious Turun Normaalikoulu. The event brought together students, teams, and enthusiasts from across the region to participate in a challenging and innovative robotics competition.

The Competition

FLL is a robotics competition designed to inspire young minds in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). The regional event at Turun Normaalikoulu was a day filled with excitement, collaboration, and technical ingenuity. Teams showcased their problem-solving skills by building and programming LEGO robots to complete a series of missions.

Highlights and Achievements

One of the most impressive performances came from the local team, pH Police, representing Turun Normaalikoulu itself. The team demonstrated exceptional skill in both robot design and programming, ultimately securing the title of regional champion. Their innovative solutions to the tasks and strong teamwork were key factors in their victory.

The competition was a fantastic opportunity for students to collaborate, experiment, and apply their STEM knowledge in a practical setting. Spectators, mentors, and fellow participants were all impressed with the level of creativity and determination displayed throughout the event.

Looking Forward

Following their win, the pH Police team will move on to the national rounds of the FLL competition, where they will face off against other top teams from across Finland. Their success in the regional competition is a testament to their hard work and dedication, and they are sure to represent Turun Normaalikoulu with pride on the national stage.

Conclusion

The FLL regional competition in Turku was a resounding success, filled with enthusiasm and innovation. It not only provided a platform for young students to engage in STEM activities but also fostered a sense of teamwork and creativity. Congratulations to all participants and especially to the winning team, pH Police, for their outstanding performance!
