At the heart of every vibrant school community lies a group of passionate individuals dedicated to telling the stories that matter most. Welcome to Norssi News, where creativity meets journalism, and every student’s voice finds a platform.

Introduction to the Journalism Club

Uncovering hidden mysteries, enlarging fascinating stories, and promoting exciting activities found in our school, we welcome all to Norssi News. Home to the knowledge seekers, detectives, and writers of our school, our club was created for the pursuit of informing the students and teachers of TIS about interesting events—past, present, and future. Through our personal website, we aim to share engaging and insightful stories that matter.

Our Mission

Norssi News is committed to fostering a dynamic environment where students can explore and express their journalistic talents. Our mission is to provide an inclusive and supportive space for students to develop their writing skills, engage in investigative reporting, and contribute to a thriving school community through thoughtful, impactful journalism. We publish a regular gazette and articles covering a wide range of topics—from school events and achievements to student opinions and creative stories. Our goal is to keep the school community informed, engaged, and inspired.

What We Do

  • Create Content: From feature articles and interviews to news reports and opinion pieces, our members produce a diverse range of content that captures the essence of school life and beyond found on our own tik tok account Norssi News and other listed socials. Whether you have a knack for writing, photography, or digital media, there’s a place for you here.
  • Develop Skills: We offer workshops and training sessions on various aspects of journalism, including news writing, editorial skills, multimedia storytelling, and ethical reporting. Our goal is to equip you with the tools and knowledge needed to excel in the world of journalism.
  • School Promotion: Our club plays a crucial role in highlighting school events, achievements, and student voices. We believe in celebrating the successes of our peers and creating a sense of unity and pride within our school community.
  • Engage in Real Reporting: Members have the opportunity to engage in real-world reporting experiences. From covering major school events and conducting interviews to investigating important issues affecting our school, we provide hands-on opportunities to practice and refine your journalistic skills.
  • Collaborate and Network: Journalism is a team effort. Our club encourages collaboration and networking among members. Whether you’re brainstorming story ideas, working on a group project, or seeking feedback on your work, you’ll find a supportive community ready to help you succeed.
